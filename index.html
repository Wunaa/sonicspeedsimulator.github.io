Why Boycott Nintendo?

Nintendo has long been a cornerstone of the gaming industry, known for its beloved franchises and innovative gameplay. However, recent actions by the company have revealed a pattern of aggressive and unjust behavior towards its fanbase. This website is dedicated to raising awareness about these issues and advocating for a boycott until Nintendo makes meaningful changes. Our goal is to show that we, as a community, will not tolerate the mistreatment of content creators, developers, and fans.

Issues with Nintendo

Modding and YouTube Videos

Modding has been an essential part of gaming culture, allowing players to enhance their experiences and showcase their creativity. However, Nintendo has taken a hardline stance against modding, issuing full DMCA takedowns against YouTube videos that showcase these modifications. These actions are not just simple copyright claims; they are legal threats that can severely damage a creator's channel. The consequences include the potential for channel strikes, reduced visibility, and a loss of income, making it harder for creators to continue their work.

Game OST Takedowns

Nintendo's games are renowned for their iconic music, which plays a significant role in the gaming experience. Despite the love for these soundtracks, Nintendo has aggressively removed videos and channels sharing game OSTs through DMCA takedowns. This is particularly concerning given that Nintendo rarely releases official soundtracks for public purchase or streaming. As a result, fans are left without legal means to enjoy the music, while creators who share these soundtracks face severe consequences. The removal of these videos not only diminishes the cultural impact of the music but also disregards the contributions of composers and fans.

Fangame Takedowns

Fangames are often a labor of love, created by fans who wish to expand on the stories and worlds they cherish. These projects are typically free and non-commercial, offering new experiences that complement the original games. Nevertheless, Nintendo has consistently targeted fangames with DMCA takedowns, forcing developers to cease all activity. This legal action not only nullifies the developers' hard work but also sends a chilling message to the fan community: creativity and passion are unwelcome. By stifling these projects, Nintendo limits the scope of innovation and prevents fans from celebrating their favorite franchises in new and exciting ways.

Other Fan-Made Content

The crackdown on fan-made content extends beyond modding and fangames. Nintendo has issued DMCA takedowns against a wide array of fan-created works, including artwork, animations, and even merchandise. These items are often made out of admiration for the company's characters and stories, with no intent to harm or profit at Nintendo's expense. Yet, the company has taken a zero-tolerance approach, disregarding the positive impact these works have on the community. This policy not only alienates fans but also stifles the creative expression that has always been a hallmark of the gaming culture.

Why Boycott?

We believe that the only way to effectively communicate our dissatisfaction with Nintendo's practices is through a boycott. By withholding our financial support, we send a clear message: we demand better treatment and respect. This boycott is not about attacking Nintendo but advocating for change. We want a future where Nintendo values its community, respects creators' rights, and fosters an environment that encourages innovation and passion.

The decision to boycott is not made lightly. It is a response to a consistent pattern of behavior that disregards the community's contributions. Our aim is to show that the fanbase will not accept the mistreatment of its members. We hope that this collective action will lead to a reevaluation of Nintendo's policies and a commitment to a more positive and supportive approach.

Alternatives and Solutions

Support Independent Creators

Independent game developers and creators often offer unique and innovative content. By supporting indie games and creators, you contribute to a more diverse gaming landscape and encourage a culture of creativity and openness. Many indie developers welcome modding and fan engagement, making them a great alternative to companies with more restrictive policies.

Engage in Advocacy

Advocacy is crucial in promoting fair use rights and protecting fan-made content. You can participate in advocacy by signing petitions, joining relevant organizations, and spreading awareness on social media. Educating others about the importance of these issues can help build a larger community of support, amplifying our collective voice in demanding change from Nintendo.

Promote Fair Use and Open Content

Fair use is a critical aspect of creative expression, allowing individuals to use copyrighted material in transformative ways. Supporting initiatives that advocate for fair use and open content helps protect creators and fans from unjust legal actions. Stay informed about fair use laws and support organizations that work towards creating a more open and inclusive creative environment.
